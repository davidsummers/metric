Use Metric v2.0: It's 1000 Times Easier
----------------------------------------------------------------
1000 millometers (1000 mm) = 1 meter (1 m) [Length Base Unit]
1000 meters (1000 m) = 1 kilameter (1 Km)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1000 milloquad (1000 mquad) = 1 quad (1 quad) [Area Base Unit] = 1 square meter (1 m^2)
1000 quad (1000 quad) = 1 kiloquad (1 Kquad)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1000 milloliters (1000 mL) = 1 liter (1 L) = 1 millovol (1 mvol)
1000 liters (1000 L) = 1 vol (1 vol) [Volume Base Unit] = 1 cubic meter (1 m^3)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1000 grams (1000 g) = 1 klug (1 kg) [Mass Base Unit]
1000 klugs (1000 kg) = 1 kilaklug (1 Kkg) = 1 metric ton
----------------------------------------------------------------
